## 🎓 Study Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Pick **one** study path and commit to it (8-week or 12-week)
- Use the **Weekly Confidence Check** to track progress
- Practice **out loud** — explain your thinking like in a real interview
- Complete **all checkpoint problems** before moving to the next week
- Review cheatsheets the **day before** interviews

❌ **Don't:**
- Jump between different study paths (creates confusion)
- Skip the mock interview practice (it's the most important!)
- Memorize answers without understanding why
- Study for more than 90 minutes at a time without a break
- Ignore the behavior/culture interview prep
