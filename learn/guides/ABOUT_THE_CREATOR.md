### Why I Created This Repository

I remember how overwhelming it was trying to figure out my career path in college — felt like everyone else had it figured out while I was just guessing. I spent way too much time applying to random jobs without understanding what different roles actually involved day-to-day.

I also got burned by a fake job offer early on (classic "buy your own equipment" scam) which is why I included extensive scam protection guidance. It's surprisingly common among students and no one really talks about it.

This repository contains everything I wish someone had explained to me back then: clear career path breakdowns, practical coding problems I actually use in my day job, salary negotiation tactics, and realistic expectations about the tech industry.
